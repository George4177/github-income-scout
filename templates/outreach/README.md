# Outreach Templates

These templates support low-pressure distribution after publishing the repository.

Rules:

- Use only where relevant and allowed.
- Do not mass-message maintainers.
- Do not promise income, merged PRs, sponsorship, or bounty awards.
- Do not imply affiliation with GitHub, Algora, or any project.
- Do not ask for credentials, payment details, private tokens, or private repository secrets.
