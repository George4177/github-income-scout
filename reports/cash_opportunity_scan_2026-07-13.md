## Decision

No external cash bounty passed all of these gates:

1. original issue and credible funding evidence;
2. open and available to this account;
3. no active assignment or duplicate implementation crowd;
4. clear, testable acceptance criteria;
5. reasonable fit for current Python, automation, documentation, and GitHub workflow capabilities;
6. no exploit, credential, fake-engagement, spam, payment-bypass, or platform-evasion risk.

The highest-value executable direction remains converting the existing Starter Audit asset into one paid delivery. The immediate action is to expose the real HTML delivery sample on GitHub Pages and continue monitoring only for qualified inbound requests or materially better original bounty issues.

No claims, comments, pull requests, wallet addresses, payment accounts, or bounty applications were created during this scan.
